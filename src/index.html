Features

  • Free

    All source codes is published under GPL license. Short time and low costs of deployment, it takes only few hours to setup everything and about two days to integrate it

  • Flexible

    You have freedom to design any decision rules on any data model, and to manage everything on the fly. Every changes reflected online, without need to re-deploy anything.

  • Rich functionality

    Define Decision and Scoring tables. Test your hypothesis with a split tests. Review and analyze all decisions. Use built-in introspection. Share access with your collaborators.

  • Simple to use

    You have freedom to design any decision rules on any data model, and to manage everything on the fly.

  • Enterprise ready

    Several PSP's use Gandalf as anti-fraud, scoring their eCommerce transactions and as decision engine to route transactions between payment gateways. Online lenders use it to manage cut-off risks and to make final decisions on customer applications. Join them and submit us a new use-case!
